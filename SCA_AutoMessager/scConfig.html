SCA AutoMessager

About this feature

SCA AutoMessager allows service to send messages in the Communication Log that are specific to each customer and site by pulling customer name, appointment date, time, and location automatically.


The tool embeds a button directly inside SCA which, when clicked, inserts a pre-formatted customer message.


Currently supported:

  • Service Center appointments
  • Body Repair Center appointments
  • Validation test drive detection
  • Waiter / Loaner logic
