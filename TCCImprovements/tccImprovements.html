TCC QOL Improvements

Teams Filter

The Teams Filter allows you to filter the team dropdown in the top right corner of TCC to only the relevant teams in Customer Support. The filtered teams are currently hard coded to Charging Support, Commerce Support, Pacific Tesla Support, Roadside, and Service Center. Any other teams can still be searched manually.


If these hard-coded teams need to be changed, please reach out to Darian George on teams.

RS Dashboard AU Filter

The AU Dashboard Filter automatically filters the Customer Connect roadside dashboard to only show dispatches in Australia (AU). It removes non-AU rows and sorts AU dispatches by ETA for quick prioritization.


Key features include:

  • Automatically removes non-AU dispatch rows from the dashboard.
  • Displays a live countdown timer with a progress bar indicating the next refresh.
  • Shows the Current Active Dispatches count inline for AU jobs, updated every second.
  • Sorts AU jobs by ETA, prioritizing negative, zero, and positive ETA values accordingly.

The script activates automatically on the roadside dashboard route when TCC QOL Improvements is enabled.
