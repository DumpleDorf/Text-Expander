Transfer Call Quick Picker

When you open the Transfer Call dialog in PACE/TCC, quick-select buttons appear beside the team search dropdown.


How it works:

  • Choose Virtual Sales Transfer, Delivery Transfer, or Energy Transfer
  • For Sales/Delivery, pick the state option (e.g. QLD Virtual Sales, WA Delivery)
  • For Energy, follow the guided flow (Customer/Installer → product → support type where needed)
  • The matching team queue is auto-selected in the dropdown

Energy transfer queues:

  • Customer + Wall Connector → AP-AU-Energy-CS-WallConnector:Voice
  • Customer + Powerwall → AP-AU-Energy-CS-Powerwall:Voice
  • Installer + Wall Connector + Onsite Installation → AP-AU-Energy-CI-DayOfInstall-WallConnector:Voice
  • Installer + Wall Connector + Onsite Maintenance/Warranty → AP-AU-Energy-CI-OSM-WallConnector:Voice
  • Installer + Wall Connector + General Support → AP-AU-Energy-CI-NotOnSite-WallConnector:Voice
  • Installer + Powerwall + Onsite Installation → AP-AU-Energy-CI-DayOfInstall-Powerwall:Voice
  • Installer + Powerwall + Onsite Maintenance/Warranty → AP-AU-Energy-CI-OSM-Powerwall:Voice
  • Installer + Powerwall + General Support → AP-AU-Energy-CI-NotOnSite-Powerwall:Voice

Team mappings are configured in transferTeams.csv. Contact Darian George on Teams if updates are needed.

Contact Center Wallboard

On the PACE Contact Center Insights wallboard, the Oldest Interaction Waiting column is colour-coded live:

  • Green — Oldest Interaction Waiting under 20 seconds
  • Yellow — 20 to 60 seconds (also colours the Waiting cell)
  • Red — over 60 seconds (also colours the Waiting cell)
  • Reverts when the value is -- (nothing waiting)

Queue names are shortened and ordered as: Roadside, Vehicle Support, Service Center, Home Charging, Body Shop.


On the Advisor Availability tab, use Open Wallboard for a fullscreen Name / State / Time board. State uses the Voice channel only (Case is ignored). Time is calculated from Last Updated and ticks live.
